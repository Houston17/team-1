Welcome

What We Do

Search Homeless works to engage, stabilize, educate, employ and house individuals and families who are homeless. Our essential services are highly effective in transforming lives because we meet individuals where they are and provide what they need to get off the streets and back on their feet.

Trust

We are credible, competent and keep our commitments.

Integrity

We do the right thing and make the right choice.

Client-Centered

We make clients the central focus of our work.

Learning Organization

We foster education, innovation and discovery.

Authentic Communication

We respectfully exchange ideas, information and perspectives.

Get Involved

We are excited to introduce our new Volunteer Program! Volunteers make an impact at SEARCH Homeless Services.
